Golden Brown & Delicious Chicken Breasts with Crispy Bread Salad

Servings: 4 servings
Total Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head radicchio (about 8 ounces)
  • 3 garlic cloves, unpeeled
  • 2 ounces Piave, Parmesan, or pecorino cheese
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 large bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts (about 2 pounds)
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 Castelvetrano olives
  • 1/4 baguette
  • 3 oil-packed anchovy fillets
  • 3 tablespoons sherry vinegar
  • Honey, for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  2. Season the chicken: Pat the chicken dry and season it evenly all over with 2½ teaspoons salt and lots of black pepper.
  3. Cook the chicken: Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a 12-inch cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat until just beginning to smoke. Place the chicken skin side down in the skillet and reduce the heat to medium. Cook, undisturbed, until the skin is golden brown, 6 to 8 minutes. Move the breasts around the pan if needed. Transfer to the oven (skin-side down) and roast until an instant-read thermometer registers 145°F, 25 to 30 minutes.
  4. Prep the salad: Tear 1 medium radicchio head into large pieces and transfer to a large bowl. Using a vegetable peeler, shave 2 ounces Piave cheese right into the radicchio. Smash 1 cup Castelvetrano olives with a mug or measuring cup, discard pits, and add to the salad. Tear 1/4 of a baguette into irregular 1-inch pieces and set aside.
  5. Baste the chicken: Smash 3 garlic cloves but don’t peel them. When the chicken is done, remove the skillet from the oven and transfer it to the stovetop. Keep the oven on. Flip the chicken breasts skin-side up. Add 3 tablespoons butter, the garlic, and 3 anchovy fillets to the skillet. Tilt the pan so the melted butter collects and, using a large spoon, continuously baste the chicken for about 1 minute. Transfer the chicken to a cutting board to rest. Add the torn bread to the butter and stir to coat. Return the skillet to the oven and bake until the croutons are crisp and golden brown, 8 to 10 minutes.
  6. Dress the salad: Add the croutons and garlic to the bowl of salad. Drizzle 3 tablespoons sherry vinegar and 3 tablespoons olive oil over the radicchio; toss well. Season with salt and black pepper. Lightly drizzle the salad with honey, tossing once to create pockets of sweetness.
  7. Serve: Carve the chicken breasts off the bone, slice them crosswise, and serve with the salad.
